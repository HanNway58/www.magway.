Transportation

The Irrawaddy River is the major transportation system in Magway Region, both in terms of volume of goods and population served. Most major towns in the region are river ports on the Irrawaddy; among them are

  • Magway
  • Pakokku
  • Minbu
  • YenangYaung
  • Chauk
  • Aunglan
  • Thayet.
